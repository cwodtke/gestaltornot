About

The Bauhaus (1919–1933) fused art, craft and industry — form follows function, built from circle, square and triangle. Gestalt psychology, its contemporary, showed the eye assembles parts into a whole. This tool reads your design through both lenses.

What the critique reads
01Proximity — how grouping implies relationship
02Similarity — shared color, shape & size
03Continuity — lines & paths the eye follows
04Closure — shapes the mind completes
05Figure / ground — subject vs. background
06Visual hierarchy — order of attention
07Alignment & grid — underlying structure
08Balance & whitespace — distribution of weight
09Contrast — tonal & color separation
